User Data Deletion Instructions

This page explains how a user can request deletion of data associated with the Teddy app.

Request method: Email us at teddycubed@gmail.com.

Please include your Facebook or Instagram account identifier used with the app, and the subject line Data Deletion Request.

  1. Send an email to teddycubed@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  3. Include enough information for us to identify your account and remove associated app data.
  4. We will confirm the request and delete relevant stored data within a reasonable timeframe.

If you no longer want Teddy to access your Facebook or Instagram data, you can also remove the app from your connected apps settings in Meta.
